

Question01: Discount Calculation System

Objective:Apply different discount strategies using @Profile.

Profiles & Logic:
-----------------
dev → NoDiscountService
Return original price

test → FixedDiscountService
Apply fixed discount (e.g., ₹100 off)

prod → PercentageDiscountService
Apply percentage discount (10% discount)

Additional Requirements:
-------------------------
Validate price (should be positive)
Ensure discount does not exceed total price
Display final price after discount
Show applied discount type
